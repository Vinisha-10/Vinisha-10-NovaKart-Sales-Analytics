# E-Commerce Sales Analytics Project

## 1. Company Overview

### Company Name

NovaKart

### Industry

E-Commerce

### Business Description

NovaKart is a fictional e-commerce company that sells products across multiple categories such as Electronics, Furniture, and Office Supplies. The company serves customers from different cities and states through an online shopping platform.

As the business grows, management needs a centralized analytics solution to monitor sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends. Currently, reports are created manually, making it difficult to make timely and data-driven decisions.

---

# 2. Business Problem

NovaKart generates thousands of sales transactions every month.

Although the company has sales data, it faces several challenges:

* Reports are created manually in Excel.
* Sales trends are difficult to identify.
* Top-performing products are not tracked effectively.
* Customer buying behavior is not analyzed.
* Regional performance comparisons take too much time.
* Management lacks a centralized dashboard for monitoring KPIs.

These challenges lead to slower decision-making and missed business opportunities.

---

# 3. Project Objective

The objective of this project is to build an end-to-end E-Commerce Sales Analytics solution using Excel, Python, SQL, PostgreSQL, Power BI, and Tableau.

The solution will:

* Analyze sales performance
* Measure profitability
* Track customer behavior
* Identify top and low-performing products
* Compare regional sales
* Automate reporting
* Provide interactive dashboards for business users

---

# 4. Stakeholders

The following stakeholders will use insights from this project:

| Stakeholder       | Business Need                      |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| CEO               | Overall business performance       |
| Sales Manager     | Revenue and sales trends           |

---

# 5. Expected Business Benefits

This analytics solution will help NovaKart:

* Reduce manual reporting time
* Improve decision-making
* Identify profitable products
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Increase customer retention
* Track business growth over time
* Support strategic planning through data-driven insights

---

# 6. Success Metrics

The project will be considered successful if:

* Dashboard loads quickly
* KPIs are accurate
* Reports are automated
* Business users can identify trends easily
* Management can make faster decisions
* Sales performance can be monitored in real time

# 7. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard will track the following KPIs:

| KPI                       | Description                                          | Business Value                            |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| Total Revenue             | Total sales generated                                | Measures overall business performance     |
| Total Profit              | Total profit earned                                  | Indicates business profitability          |
| Total Orders              | Number of customer orders                            | Tracks sales volume                       |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Average amount spent per order                       | Helps understand customer spending habits |
| Profit Margin             | Percentage of profit from revenue                    | Measures operational efficiency           |
| Repeat Customer Rate      | Percentage of customers who purchased more than once | Measures customer loyalty                 |
| Top Selling Products      | Products generating the highest sales                | Helps inventory and marketing decisions   |
| Top Performing Regions    | States or cities with the highest revenue            | Supports regional business strategy       |
